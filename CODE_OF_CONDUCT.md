# Code of Conduct | منشور اخلاقی

## قانون اساسی نوین - Contributor Covenant

### Our Pledge | تعهد ما

We, the contributors and maintainers of this project, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Ethnic background (Persian, Azerbaijani, Kurdish, Baloch, Arab, Turkmen, and all others)
- Religious belief (Shia, Sunni, Zoroastrian, Jewish, Christian, Baha'i, non-religious, and all others)
- Gender identity and expression
- Sexual orientation
- Age
- Disability
- Physical appearance
- Political affiliation (within democratic bounds)
- Location (inside Iran, diaspora)
- Level of experience

ما، مشارکت‌کنندگان و نگهدارندگان این پروژه، متعهد می‌شویم که مشارکت در جامعه ما تجربه‌ای بدون آزار برای همه باشد، بدون توجه به پیشینه قومی، اعتقادات مذهبی، هویت جنسیتی، گرایش جنسی، سن، معلولیت، ظاهر جسمانی، وابستگی سیاسی، مکان یا سطح تجربه.

---

### Our Standards | معیارهای ما

#### Positive Behavior | رفتار مثبت

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Protecting the anonymity and safety of vulnerable contributors
- Acknowledging the complexity of Iran's political situation

- استفاده از زبان خوشامدگو و فراگیر
- احترام به دیدگاه‌ها و تجربیات متفاوت
- پذیرش انتقاد سازنده
- تمرکز بر بهترین‌ها برای جامعه
- همدلی با سایر اعضای جامعه
- حفاظت از ناشناسی و امنیت مشارکت‌کنندگان آسیب‌پذیر

#### Unacceptable Behavior | رفتار غیرقابل‌قبول

- Personal attacks or insults based on any protected characteristic
- Doxxing or revealing private information
- Trolling, inflammatory comments, or sustained disruption
- Advocating for violence or illegal activity
- Harassment in any form
- Ethnic, religious, or gender-based discrimination
- Attempts to identify anonymous contributors
- Sharing information that could endanger inside-Iran contributors

- حملات شخصی یا توهین
- افشای اطلاعات خصوصی
- ایجاد اختلال مداوم
- ترویج خشونت
- آزار به هر شکل
- تبعیض قومی، مذهبی یا جنسیتی
- تلاش برای شناسایی مشارکت‌کنندگان ناشناس
- به اشتراک‌گذاری اطلاعاتی که می‌تواند مشارکت‌کنندگان داخل ایران را به خطر بیندازد

### Special Considerations for This Project | ملاحظات ویژه

#### Political Sensitivity | حساسیت سیاسی

This project involves deeply held political beliefs. We expect:

- Critique of *ideas*, not *people*
- Acknowledgment that there are multiple valid perspectives on governance
- Patience with those learning about constitutional principles
- Recognition that trauma from political persecution affects many contributors

#### Safety First | امنیت در اولویت

- Never pressure anyone to reveal their identity
- Use secure communication channels when discussing sensitive matters
- Report any attempts to identify anonymous contributors immediately
- Be mindful that some contributors face real danger

#### Inclusive Deliberation | مشورت فراگیر

- Actively seek input from underrepresented groups
- Acknowledge when majority perspectives may overshadow minorities
- Create space for voices that are often silenced
- Consider the needs of those who cannot openly participate

### Enforcement | اجرا

#### Reporting | گزارش‌دهی

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to:

- **Email**: [conduct@qanun-e-asasi.org] (PGP available)
- **Anonymous form**: [Link to be added]
- **Direct message**: Any Steering Committee member

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

#### Consequences | پیامدها

1. **Warning** | اخطار
   - Private written warning
   - Explanation of violation

2. **Temporary Ban** | محرومیت موقت
   - 7-30 day suspension from participation
   - No interaction with community members

3. **Permanent Ban** | محرومیت دائم
   - Permanent removal from project
   - Reserved for severe or repeated violations

4. **Security Alert** | هشدار امنیتی
   - If a contributor's safety is threatened
   - Immediate protective measures
   - Coordination with trusted security organizations
