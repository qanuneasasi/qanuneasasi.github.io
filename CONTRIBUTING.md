## Step 1: Create a GitHub Account

GitHub is a free website where we store and collaborate on this constitution. Think of it like Google Docs but for larger projects.

1. Go to **[github.com/signup](https://github.com/signup)**

## Step 4: Make Your Changes

When the editor opens:

1. **GitHub will ask to "fork" the repository** - Click "Fork this repository"
   - This creates your own copy to edit

2. **Edit the text**
   - Find the section you want to change
   - The files use Markdown format (simple text formatting)
   - Change the text as needed

3. **Important**: Edit BOTH Farsi and English sections if changing article text

## Step 5: Describe Your Changes

After editing, scroll down. You'll see two boxes:

1. **Title** (short description):
   - Example: "Fix typo in Article 8" or "Add clause about disability rights"

2. **Description** (explain WHY):
   - Why is this change needed?
   - What sources support it? (other constitutions, international law, etc.)
   - Any concerns the community should discuss?

**Good example:**
```
Title: Add explicit protection for disabled persons in Article 9

Description:
The current Article 9 on equality doesn't explicitly mention disability.
This change adds "disability" to the list of protected characteristics.

This aligns with:
- UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (which Iran signed)
- Germany's Basic Law Article 3(3)
- South Africa's Constitution Section 9

This addresses the needs of approximately 3 million Iranians with disabilities.
```

## Step 6: Submit Your Proposal

1. Click **"Propose changes"** (green button)
2. On the next page, click **"Create pull request"**
3. Add any additional comments
4. Click **"Create pull request"** again

**Congratulations!** Your proposal is now submitted for community review!

---

## Step 7: Participate in the Discussion

After submitting:

1. **Watch for comments** - Community members may ask questions or suggest improvements
2. **Respond thoughtfully** - Explain your reasoning
3. **Make updates if needed** - You can edit your proposal based on feedback
4. **Vote happens after 7-14 days** - Community votes on whether to accept
